Coffee Brewing Fundamentals

Great coffee starts with quality beans and proper technique. Understanding the basics will transform your daily cup from ordinary to extraordinary.

Essential Equipment

  • Coffee grinder (burr preferred)
  • Digital scale for precise measurements
  • Gooseneck kettle for controlled pouring
  • Timer for consistent brewing

Popular Brewing Methods

Each brewing method brings out different flavors in your coffee. Start with one method and master it before exploring others.

Pour Over

Clean, bright flavors with full control over extraction. Perfect for highlighting single-origin coffees and their unique characteristics.

French Press

Full-bodied coffee with rich oils and sediment. Simple method that produces consistently strong, flavorful coffee.

Brewing ratio guide
Method Coffee to Water Ratio Brew Time
Pour Over 1:16 3-4 minutes
French Press 1:15 4 minutes

Brewing Success Tips

Small details make a big difference in your final cup. Follow these guidelines for consistently great coffee.

  1. Use freshly roasted beans (within 2-4 weeks of roast date)
  2. Grind beans just before brewing
  3. Use filtered water heated to 195-205°F
  4. Maintain consistent coffee-to-water ratios
